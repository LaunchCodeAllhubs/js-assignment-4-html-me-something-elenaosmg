Books About Kids

There are tones of books and resources for parents nowadays! This page is a digest of my recent picks that I enjoied reading and found very usufull.

Books In Russian

  • ✎ Happy Child, by Andrew Kurpatov
  • ✎ Secret Support: An Emotional Connection In A Child's Life, by Ludmila Petranovskaya

Books In English

  • ✎ The Educated Child, by by William J. Bennett, Chester E. Finn Jr., John T. E. Cribb Jr.
  • ✎ When Your Child Drives You Crazy, by Eda J.LeShan

The book details how a child's brain is formed, reveals the secrets of child's emotional sphere development and social relationships evolution. It gives the reader understanding of kids ability to think and make meaningful decisions and tells how to deal with specific challenges and how to balance parenting tasks with happiness of parenthood.

The book is based on the scientific theory of attachment. It shows how dependence and helplessness of a baby turns into maturity, how our love and care year after year form a secret support for a child personality. Explaining what really stands behind children's "whims", "spoiledness", "aggression", "bad character", the book prompts on how to help kids grow and develop without wasting energy on fighting for parents love.

As a foreignor I found this book very helpfull in understanding how to enreach kids journey in American school and make it more profound. The details on what and when children should be studying and the kind of work they should be doing together with essential reading lists makes it easier to judge if the quality of education the child gets meets the standarts set in a book.

You learn from this book how uncertain our kids are and how much it is when parents know how to asure kids in their capability and be realistic in expectations, how to cultivate responsibility in our kids and what hurts kids most of all. It gives lots of practical advice to parents on how to respond when child is scared, nagging, rude, or accident-prone and this book also helps parents understand their own feelings and emotions and teaches remembering of taking care of ourselves.
